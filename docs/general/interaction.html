General Interaction¶

Commands¶

The VGMC bot responds to commands prefixed with a period: .. If the command is valid, then the bot will respond. All commands that do not exist will simply be ignored.

Channels¶

The bot will respond to commands in the same channel that they are sent – assuming the bot has permissions to use the channel. For text-heavy commands, it’s recommended to PM the bot.
